### Conceptual Document Model

The Conceptual Document Model is the intellectual foundation of OMI.

Unlike traditional word processors, OMI treats a manuscript as a structured scholarly object rather than formatted text. Every element—from citations and figures to annotations and review comments—is represented semantically, enabling flexible publishing workflows and long-term interoperability.

**Document:** `docs/document-model.md`
